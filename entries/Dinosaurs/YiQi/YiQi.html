Info

        Yi Qi was descoverd in china in 2007, and it may well be the closest we ever got to real life dragons. Its name meaning "Strange Wing" is in refrence to the membraine it would have had on its wings in life. Before the discovery of Yi Qi its relitives were thought to have just had unnatraully long fingers, however after examining its fossails it was realised that their was an entire family of dragon winged dinosaurs. Examination of Yi Qi's wings has suggested that it wasent a particulaurly good flier, but it could still climb and glide effectivly to get around.
