Late Cretaceous

Location:
North America

Diet:
Herbivore

Weight:
1200kg

Info

        The first therizinosaur to be discribed from North America. Nothronychus is often over looked for its eastern relietives, which are known to be larger. Despite this it would have been a formidable herbivore, using its large claws to defend itself and forage for plant matter. Another note worthy characteristic is its large pot belly, this would have helped it break down tough plant matter that other creatures would have struggled to consume. Its name meaning "Slothful Claw" pays homage to both its key features.
