Info

        Cuenca in the modern day is a province in Spain, but back in the Cretaceous period it was the home of the deadly Concavenator. This dinosaur is best identified by the fin it possesses above its hips. There are many theories as to what the purpose of the fin was. It might have been used for temperature control, or display, some even think it may have been used to store fat like the hump of a camel. unfortunately we will likely never know what its exact function was, perhaps it was used for all the aforementioned theories, or maybe none of them. Only with the discovery and research of more specimens could we ever hope to know more about this unique Theropod dinosaur.
